For the Perceptive Music Listener

Welcome! I’m so honored that you’re here, checking out a little project that I like to call Through The Noise which originated as a class project in college. You may be wondering, “Why ‘Through The Noise’? What’s that supposed to mean?” I’m glad you asked ;) It’s my simple observation that of the millions of songs out there, relatively few cut through the noise of complacency. And thus it is my heart to shine the spotlight on those precious songs that, instead, shine light on the nuances of this crazy thing called life.

About

Noah Wiley

What’s up? In case you’re curious, here’s a 30,000-foot look at me. As you might have already gathered, I love music, digging deeper, and communicating thoughts with others. I love music so much that I went to school for business, moved to Nashville, and hustled to work in the music industry that has shaped me more than I’ll probably ever fully understand.

The reality, though, is that in the heat of the competitive nature of the music industry, it gradually became a disturbingly deep idol in my heart. While I was ignorant to this reality, the Holy Spirit (aka God aka Jesus (hmu if that doesn’t make sense)) told me to let go of the music industry. That was hard to hear, but even more so I was grateful for “permission” to let go of this thing that never reciprocated my devotion.

So these days I still live in Nashville and I work at a smoothie shop called E+Rose. And instead of hunting down the next possible-maybe-opportunity, I’m focused on living the Life for which God made you and I. And truly, it’s so much better.
