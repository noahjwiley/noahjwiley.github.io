For the Perceptive Music Listener

Hey guys, welcome! Through The Noise exists to promote substantial music that adds value to the listener. Too often we hear songs that elevate complacency amidst our complex world so, in my playlists and blog, you will find none of that. Instead, I want to show you music that adds something meaningful to the world rather than joining the rest of the commotion. We’re cutting Through The Noise.

About

Noah Wiley, Playlist Curator and Primary Contributor

Hey, I’m a business administration student at Messiah University and will be graduating in May of this year (2023). Inspired by Jon Foreman, I always thought it would be cool to drop out of college, but it’s starting to look like a lost dream.

Really though, school does not define me. I’m very much interested in business, but my other passions include my faith, friends, and football (that’s accidental alliteration right there). Oh, and music!

After several years of listening to music on my own Spotify account (forget sharing one with your brothers), I started to notice some common themes amongst the songs that I listened to. Often times this theme would stretch into a diverse set of genres, but its impact on me remained. Finding value in these songs, I began making playlists to compile them and planned to one day share them for the benefit of others. Well, here we are today and I’m glad to have your interest! For real, my hope is that these playlists and written thoughts will serve as a medium between you and the blessing of these artist’s work.
